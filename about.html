About Us

Pastasciutta On The Coast Restaurant is located in Paradise Point 23 King Street. PotC refurbished an heritage-listed site, with influences of traditional Italian-inspired style, to create a serene dining enclave in the Northern Gold Coast. PotC serves a degustation menu. The degustation is unique, based on the Italian philosophy of using natural flavours, enhanced by classic Italian technique and utilising the freshest possible ingredients.

We have been in our current location since 1999, with previous restaurants located in Western Sydney. We have been in the Italian Restaurant business for over 25 years. Your hosts Roberto and Rosa invite you to enjoy our mouth watering traditional Italian food plus our range of fresh seafood and home made desserts.
